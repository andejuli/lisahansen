About me:

With over 35 years of experience as a pianist and educator, I bring a lifelong passion for music to every performance and collaboration. I've spent 20 years teaching privately and 15 years as a faculty member at a local college, mentoring students of all ages and skill levels. Beyond teaching, I thrive on collaboration and have had the job of contributing to operas, musicals, recitals, weddings, parties, and even cruise ships. Whether it's bringing life to a stage production or creating the perfect atmosphere for an event, I love connecting with audiences through music I have my own portable equipment: piano, amp and microphone.
